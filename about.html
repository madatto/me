About Me

I’m Maddie, a fourth-year Film and Media Studies student pursuing a minor in Professional Writing at UC Santa Barbara. From coding to music composition, I am passionate about all things creative; I thrive on integrating the art of design and storytelling into the dynamic spheres of gaming and entertainment.

Through my internship with Marginalized Genders in Gaming (MGG) at UCSB, I have leveraged social media to tailor my voice and visuals to engage with a gaming-interested audience, advocating for change in the industry to foster inclusivity for all individuals. My experience spans events, marketing, partnerships, and outreach within the gaming sphere.

My media experience extends to cinematography, photography, graphic design, screenwriting, and game design. I'm constantly exploring innovative ways to tell stories and engage new audiences!

Additionally, I have experience in data collection, analysis, and research. As an Undergraduate Research Assistant, I collaborated with professors and PhD students to collect, organize, and analyze data on user-AI interaction. I've also conducted user research and analyzed sales data in my own small business endeavors. Moving forward, I aspire to further combine my enthusiasm for the gaming industry with my creative abilities and analytical skills.

If you're interested in connecting further, please don't hesitate to reach out or take a look at my portfolio for a deeper dive into my work. I'm excited about the opportunity to connect with you!
