Documentary: Unconditional

January-March 2024

This documentary follows the production of the new play Everybody's Favorite Mothers by James Still. This is a collaboration between the UCSB Film Department and the Department of Theater and Dance.

I worked as Director of Photography and shot for 6-7 hours each week for 10 weeks. I gained experience with the Sony FX6, lavaliers, recording devices, and lighting equipment. Additionally, I conducted interviews and set up/broke down equipment daily. It was an amazing experience watching such an important play come to life over a short time span!
